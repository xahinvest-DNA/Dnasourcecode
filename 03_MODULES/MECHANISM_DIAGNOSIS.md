# Mechanism Diagnosis Module

## Responsibility
Produce one leading hidden poverty mechanism hypothesis from intake data.

## Inputs
- Structured intake record
- Communication DNA analysis
- Prior cycle history when available

## Outputs
- Leading mechanism hypothesis
- Old belief statement
- Hidden prohibition or safety issue, if present
- Confidence note
- Risk of self-sabotage note

## Non-Goals
- Multiple competing diagnoses in MVP
- Final psychological truth claim
- Long explanatory reports

## Boundary Rule
- Diagnosis returns one operational hypothesis that is good enough to drive the next step.
