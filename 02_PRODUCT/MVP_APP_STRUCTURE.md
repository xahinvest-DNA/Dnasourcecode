# MVP App Structure

## App Sections
1. Entry framing
2. Intake capture
3. Mechanism diagnosis
4. Old cycle presentation
5. New cycle construction
6. One micro-action assignment
7. Check-in capture
8. Progress history

## Structure Rules
- The MVP is a mini-app, not a general chat shell.
- Each section must hand off one structured output to the next.
- The app should be usable in short sessions.
- The first version is documentation- and structure-driven, with implementation deferred until the technical model is fixed.
