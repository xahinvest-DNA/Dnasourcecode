# Product Scope

## In Scope
- One user flow focused on money/income difficulty.
- Intake from natural-language user input.
- Diagnosis of a hidden poverty mechanism hypothesis.
- Presentation of the old cycle.
- Construction of a new admissible belief and new cycle.
- Generation of exactly one bounded micro-action.
- Structured post-action check-in.
- Progress history across completed cycles.

## Out of Scope
- Clinical or therapeutic claims.
- Broad life coaching across many domains in the first version.
- Multiple simultaneous action plans in one cycle.
- Rich habit systems, gamification, communities, or content libraries.
- Detailed financial planning, budgeting, or investment tooling.
- Open-ended chat as the primary product form.

## Scope Rule
- Every packet and every feature must strengthen the one-cycle trainer before expanding surface area.
